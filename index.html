About Me

The most laid back person with chronic anxiety you will ever meet. I love snowboarding, snow and mountains are where I feel more comfortable than anywhere else in the world. I also absolutely love Zelda games, so instead of talking more about myself I think I'd rather provide Zelda lore in a nutshell. Don't worry, it won't be nearly as verbose as you might think.

The Legend of Zelda is very complex when it comes to timelines but the story itself is quite simple. Discovered in 'Skyward Sword' princess Zelda is actually the reincarnation of the goddess "Hylia." The big bad guy in Syward Sword's name is Demise. As the Link from the 'Skyward Sword' game defeats Demise he is tossed into the abyss of death, but he proclaims that his evil spirit will live on and plague the land of Hyrule forever.

This is where the reincarnation of Demise, known as Ganondorf, comes from and why he appears in 'Ocarina of Time' and other games in the series as the biggest evil to threaten the land since Demise himself. There is speculation on whether every Link is a reincarnation of the hero chosen by Hylia (Zelda) or whether Link is simply a name passed down in the family's of the knights of Hyrule. The point that is agreed upon though, is that Link is supposed to represent a rather average person capable of great things such as saving the world.
